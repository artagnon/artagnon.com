Recall the homology group $H_n = \frac{\texttt{ker } \del(n)}{\texttt{im } \del(n + 1)}$ with the property $\del^2 = 0$. In homoglogical algebra, a chain complex is a family ${C_n}_{n \in \mathfrak{Z}}$ is a family of $R-modules$.

$$ \begin{xy} \xymatrix{ \ellipses C_{n + 1} \ar[r] C_n \ar[r]^{d_n} \ellipses } \end{xy} $$
